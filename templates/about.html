About Seekho Sakhi

Seekho Sakhi is an innovative hybrid web application combining a prediction model, chatbot, text-to-speech (TTS), and speech-to-text (STT) features. Designed with the goal of keeping women safe, it empowers users by providing timely awareness of crimes happening around them.

Our platform predicts potential crime trends based on city-wise statistics and reflects them in an easy-to-understand format. Through the chatbot, users can interact naturally, receive safety tips, and access essential helpline numbers.

By leveraging real-time insights, predictive analytics, and interactive communication, Seekho Sakhi ensures that women stay informed, vigilant, and prepared—turning data into actionable safety knowledge.

Key Features:
- Crime prediction based on city-level data
- Interactive chatbot for queries and guidance
- Voice-enabled TTS and STT for accessibility
- Quick access to emergency helplines

Important Helplines: Police - 100 | Women Helpline - 181 | Child Helpline - 1098
